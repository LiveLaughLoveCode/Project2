Dive into the exhilarating Circle Chase Game, where quick reflexes and strategic moves are your keys to success. In this captivating game, you control a nimble red circle, evading the relentless pursuit of a blue circle while collecting valuable yellow dots. Each dot you snag not only boosts your score but also fuels the thrill of the chase.

The gameplay is straightforward yet addictive: dodge the blue circle, gather yellow dots, and rack up points. With each dot collected, your score climbs, pushing you to outdo yourself in every round. The high score feature adds a competitive edge, preserving your best performances and challenging you to break your own records.
